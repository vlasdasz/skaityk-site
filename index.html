Skaityk

Lithuanian reader & translator

Read Lithuanian texts with built-in word-by-word translation to English. Tap any word to see its meaning. A reading tool for anyone learning Lithuanian or exploring Lithuanian literature and news.

Support

For questions, feedback, or bug reports, open an issue on GitHub.

Privacy Policy

Skaityk does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The app fetches publicly available news articles and uses Google Translate for word-by-word translation. No user accounts, analytics, tracking, or advertising are used. All cached data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to third parties.
